About JJ Nexus Global

Who We Are

JJ Nexus Global Consultancy (a unit of Nexus Educational Charitable Trust) is one of the leading educational consultancies in India. Established in 2023 and registered under the Government of Kerala, we have quickly acquired acute expertise and excellence in the field of education.

We represent about 100 reputed colleges across the country and refer thousands of candidates every year. With a cogent network of offices located in major cities of India—including our head offices in Bangalore and Vijayawada—we maintain a strong presence in Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and the UAE.
